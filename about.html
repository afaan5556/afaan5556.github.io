WHERE I'M FROM


I grew up in Karachi, Pakistan, a city with an estimated population of 23 million. Karachi was my home through high-school after which I left Pakistan for undergraduate studies.

My parents still live in Karachi, and I visit them every year.


EDUCATIONAL EXPERIENCE

I attended middle and high school in Karachi, before heading to Montreal for an undergraduate degree in Mechanical Engineering at McGill University. After many hot and humid summers in Karachi, the freezing Montreal winters took some adjusting to!

I then completed a Masters degree in Engineering Management at Rochester Institute of Technology in upstate New York, before moving to San Francisco for my job at Arup. I have since completed a masters module course in Smart Cities at Imperial College London.
